big data

We are surrounded by data. Every time we use social media, buy something online, or even search for information, we are creating data. Because there’s so much of it, and because it comes in many forms, we call it big data.

Big data is a kind of data collection which is beyond the capability of traditional database in acquisition, storage, management and analysis. The strategic significance of big data technology lies not in mastering huge data information, but in processing these data to make the data more meaningful.

artificial intelligence

Artificial intelligence can simulate people's consciousness and thinking process, and think like people. Research in this field includes language recognition, image recognition, natural language processing and expert system. In general,one of the main goals of AI research is to enable machines to perform complex tasks that people usually need to do.

advantage：

Unlike humans, machines don't get tired. They work very efficiently, even if they have to work for hours.In some situations where personal safety is vulnerable, artificial intelligence can be used. Artificial intelligence will gradually replace people's tedious work and gradually liberate people's hands, but in this way, a large number of people will lose their jobs.

disadvantage：

AI needs a lot of memory, computing power and big data to work, and may not be suitable for low-power devices. Machines have no aesthetic and emotional sense. Although machines can imitate human painting, poetry, music and other artistic styles, and can reproduce and create computer art works, they don't really know what beauty is.

graduation project

As for my graduation project, the topic is completing the design and implementation of kv storage engine based on Raft protocol, a consensus algorithm for managing a replicated log. Firstly I'll implement Raft protocol. It includes leader election, log replication, persistent storage, log compression. And the last one is used to finish the snapshoot function. Next I'll build a key/value service on the top of Raft. Then I will “shard” the service over multiple replicated state machines for higher performance. At last, I need to provide my own API to enable data addition, deletion, modification, query and other operations.

mapreduce

The mapreduce system includes some worker processes and a coordinator. The worker process needs to call application Map and Reduce functions and handles reading and writing files. The coordinator process that hands out tasks to workers and copes with failed workers. I'll be building something similar to the MapReduce paper. Users only need to specify their own Map and Reduce applications, the system will start up in a parallel way to get higher performance.
